Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. All your transactions, accounts, and budgets are stored locally on your device — there's no account, no sign-up, and no server. Nothing leaves your phone unless you explicitly enable a cloud AI provider for receipt scanning in Settings.

How do recurring transactions work?

In Settings → Transactions, choose Auto-Create (due transactions are added automatically) or Manually Confirm (you'll get a notification and a Pay Today button to confirm each one yourself).

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings → Backup & Reset to create an encrypted backup to your personal iCloud Drive or local files, and to restore from a previous backup. You can also enable automatic backups.

Can I use multiple currencies?

Yes. Every account can have its own currency, exchange rates sync automatically, and your dashboard shows a converted net worth in your chosen default currency.

How does receipt scanning work?

Enable AI Parsing in Settings to scan receipts and auto-fill transaction details using an on-device model by default. You can optionally switch to a cloud provider (Google, OpenAI, Anthropic, or a custom endpoint) if you provide your own API key.

How do I secure the app?

Settings → Security lets you set a PIN and enable Face ID / Touch ID. This is handled entirely by iOS — Ex-Tracka never has access to your biometric data.
